FAQ

Questions About Shield Dat Azz™

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime, no cancellation fee, no penalty, no guilt trips. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. Simple cancel button in the member portal — that's it.
Does Shield Dat Azz™ create an attorney-client relationship?
Membership alone does not. A formal attorney-client relationship requires a separate engagement agreement signed by Jamaree R. James, Esq. Your membership gives you legal resources, monitoring, and initial consultations — full representation is handled separately, usually on contingency.
What happens if my scan finds a violation worth suing over?
Members who qualify for litigation get priority intake and the contingency structure — $0 upfront. If we take your case, attorney fees come from the violator under the statute (§ 1692k FDCPA, § 1681n FCRA, § 227 TCPA). Your membership doesn't cost you anything extra for litigation — it actually fast-tracks you into it.
Is the founding rate really locked in forever?
Yes. Your founding rate of $29/mo is locked in as long as your membership stays active. Cancel and come back, you pay the current rate. Stay active, your rate never goes up — even when we raise it to $49/mo for everyone else.
What exactly does the monthly violation scan check?
Three-law check every 30 days: (1) FCRA scan of your credit report for errors, re-aging, mixed files, and failed investigations; (2) FDCPA check on any debt collection contacts you've logged; (3) TCPA review if you've received unsolicited calls or texts. Full report in your inbox within 48 hours of the monthly scan date.
How does the $250 referral bonus work?
Refer someone who becomes a Shield Dat Azz™ member OR a paying client for litigation or consulting. Once their first payment clears, you get a $250 Amazon gift card within 30 days. No cap on referrals — refer 4 people and your membership is free for the year.
